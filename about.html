Who we are?

We are a plastic manufacturing company which started off with a single machine that produced a particular product, i.e, a jug under the company name Sukumar Polymers. As we grew, we started adding products and the necessary machinery for the same. As a result we expanded to create two more companies that is, Himalayan Thermoplast and Blossom Polymers for stepping into the production of other household plastic items. Today we are a part of these three companies which produces over 40 distinct items.

We are a household furniture manufacturing company promising 100% quality assurance and service.

Produced over 2 ton of plastic furniture last year which was distributed over a road-map of 20,000 sq. kms.
